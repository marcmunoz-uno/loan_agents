## DSCR Calculation

Lima One calculates DSCR on **gross monthly rent** (not net):

```
DSCR = Gross Monthly Rent / (Principal + Interest + Taxes + Insurance + HOA)
```

- **Short-term rental (Airbnb/VRBO):** Use 75% of projected gross rental income. Require 12-month rental history or STR market analysis from a licensed appraiser.
- **Proforma rent:** Acceptable if property is vacant at origination. Use current lease if occupied. Appraiser must include market rent estimate on Form 1007.
- **Min DSCR 1.10:** Below 1.10, deal is not eligible. LendingOne or a no-DSCR product is the redirect.

## Reserves Requirement

**6 months PITI** of the subject property, verified. Sources:
- Business or personal bank statements (2 most recent months, all pages)
- Retirement accounts: 60% haircut on vested balance
- Gift funds: NOT eligible for reserves
- Self-directed IRAs: eligible with custodian statement

---

## Entity Vesting

If vesting in an LLC, LP, or trust:
- Operating agreement (all pages + executed amendment if recent)
- EIN letter (IRS Form SS-4 or CP-575)
- Certificate of good standing from state of formation (< 90 days old)
- Title must vest in entity name exactly as it appears on operating agreement
- Personal guaranty required from all members with ≥ 20% ownership

## Standard Conditions Package (DSCR)

**Prior to Submission (PTSU):**
1. Tri-merge credit pull (Lima One orders — do not use broker's credit)
2. Executed purchase contract or payoff statement (refi)
3. Preliminary title report (Lima One preferred title)
4. Completed Lima One DSCR application (portal)
5. Entity docs (if LLC vesting): ops agreement + EIN letter + good standing

**Prior to Close (PTC):**
6. Full DSCR appraisal — Form 1025 or 1004 + 1007 market rent schedule
   - AVM allowed if loan ≤ $400K AND FICO ≥ 720 AND LTV ≤ 70% (waiver required)
7. Insurance binder: replacement cost ≥ loan amount, company rated A- or better
8. Lease agreement (if occupied) or market rent letter (if vacant)
9. Bank statements — 2 months, all pages, reserves verification
10. Property tax current — no delinquencies

**Prior to Funding (PTF):**
11. Final title commitment (Lima One title or approved alternate)
12. Executed note + deed of trust
13. Evidence of insurance with Lima One as loss payee (ATIMA)

## Underwriting Hot Buttons

- **DSCR floor is hard:** 1.09 DSCR will decline. Do not submit with DSCR < 1.10.
- **Short-term rental proration:** STR income is haircut to 75% — verify this doesn't push DSCR below floor before submitting.
- **Entity docs:** Incomplete LLC docs are the #1 cause of stalls at Lima One. Get the full operating agreement with all signatures before you start the file.
- **Condo questionnaire:** Warrantable condos require lender-specific condo questionnaire. Budget 2–3 days for HOA response.
- **6 months reserves — no exceptions:** Lima One will decline or suspend files missing verified reserves. Do not submit without bank statements.
- **Rural / large lot:** Properties > 5 acres or rural designation will be declined. Run APN check for zoning before ordering appraisal.
