## Program Differentiator

LendingOne has the **most flexible DSCR program** among our lender partners:
- 620 FICO minimum (lowest in the stack)
- 0.95 min DSCR — captures deals other DSCR lenders decline
- **No-DSCR variant** for properties where rent doesn't cover PITI (great for below-market-rent legacy leases, value-add transitional properties)
- 5–8 unit eligible (commercial lite underwriting)
- Portfolio lending: cross-collateralize multiple properties for blanket loan

---

## DSCR Calculation

Standard: same formula as industry (gross rent / PITI+HOA).

**No-DSCR product:**
- DSCR waiver available if LTV ≤ 75% (purchase) or ≤ 65% (cash-out)
- Borrower must demonstrate global cash flow via tax returns or bank statements
- Rate add-on: 50–75bps
- Max loan $2M under no-DSCR
- Not available for 5–8 unit

## Standard Conditions Package

**Prior to Submission:**
1. LendingOne application (broker portal)
2. Purchase contract or refi payoff
3. Entity docs

**Prior to Close:**
4. DSCR appraisal with rent schedule (Form 1007)
5. Lease agreement or market rent letter
6. 2 months bank statements
7. Insurance binder (landlord/dwelling policy)
8. Title commitment
9. Tax current confirmation
10. For no-DSCR: 2 years personal tax returns + 3 months bank statements (global cash flow underwriting)

## Hot Buttons

- **620 FICO is the opening for distressed credit:** If a borrower was declined at Kiavi (640 min) or Lima One (660 min), bring them to LendingOne. They will price the risk in instead of declining.
- **No-DSCR for below-market leases:** Legacy tenant paying $800/mo on a property where market is $1,200? LendingOne no-DSCR at 65% LTV may work. Other lenders use actual rent, not market — so they'd decline on DSCR.
- **5–8 unit opens bigger deals:** SFR DSCR lenders top out at 4 units. LendingOne goes to 8. For 5-unit deals, this is often the only option without going to a commercial bridge lender.
- **Portfolio blanket loans:** If borrower wants to cross-collateralize 5–10 rentals, LendingOne is the strongest partner for it. This is a real differentiator vs. Kiavi/Lima One.
