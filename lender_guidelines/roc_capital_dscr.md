## Program Differentiator

Roc Capital specializes in **rental portfolio loans** and cross-collateralized blanket mortgages. For single-asset DSCR they are competitive, but their real edge is portfolio lenders looking to consolidate multiple properties into one loan.

They also offer a unique **portfolio cross-collateral facility** where an investor can pledge equity in stabilized rentals to fund additional acquisitions without refinancing each property.

## Eligible Property Types

SFR, 2–4 unit, warrantable condos, townhomes. No 5+ unit, no manufactured, no rural, no commercial. Rural = any property > 5 acres or flagged as agricultural use.

## 6 Months Reserves — Hard Requirement

Roc Capital requires **6 months PITI verified** with no exceptions. This is the single most common reason files stall. Do not submit without verified reserves.

Sources:
- Personal/business bank statements (2 months, all pages)
- Investment account (60% haircut on non-retirement; 60% haircut on retirement vested balance)
- Gift funds: NOT eligible

## Hot Buttons

- **6 months reserves is the #1 kill shot:** Roc will suspend (not decline — they are polite about it) if reserves are unverifiable. Get bank statements before you start the file.
- **Rural = automatic decline:** Check zoning on the county assessor's website. "Rural" means more than acreage — it can be triggered by agricultural zoning codes even on suburban lots.
- **HOA questionnaire timeline:** For condos, the HOA questionnaire takes 10–15 days. Order it immediately at submission or you'll miss close.
- **Portfolio cross-collateral is a real product:** If your borrower has 5+ rentals with equity, Roc Capital is the first call for portfolio line of credit or blanket refi.
- **Prepay required:** Unlike Lima One (no prepay option available), Roc requires a prepay on all DSCR loans. Factor this into the borrower's hold period analysis.
