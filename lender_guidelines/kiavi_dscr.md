## Eligible Property Types

- SFR
- 2–4 unit
- Townhomes (no HOA litigation, < 15% delinquency)

**Not eligible:** Condos (Kiavi does not do DSCR condos — use Lima One), manufactured, rural, 5+ unit, commercial.

---

## DSCR Calculation

```
DSCR = Gross Monthly Rent / (PITI + HOA)
```

- **Market rent:** Kiavi accepts market rent from licensed appraiser on Form 1007. Property does not need to be leased.
- **Min DSCR = 1.00:** Kiavi prices down to 1.00 DSCR. Below 1.00, file routes to LendingOne no-DSCR product.
- **Max 10 financed properties** per borrower (Fannie/Freddie limit doesn't apply, but Kiavi internal limit does).

## Reserves

3 months PITI verified. Sources accepted: business/personal bank statements (2 months, all pages). Retirement at 60%. Gift NOT acceptable for reserves.

---

## Standard Conditions Package

**Prior to Submission:**
1. Kiavi online application (kiavi.com broker portal)
2. Executed purchase contract
3. Borrower entity docs (LLC: ops agreement + EIN)

**Prior to Close:**
4. DSCR appraisal with market rent (Form 1007)
5. Current lease or market rent letter
6. 2 months bank statements (all pages)
7. Insurance binder: landlord/dwelling policy, replacement cost ≥ loan
8. Title commitment (Kiavi preferred title vendors)
9. Current property tax receipt or confirmation taxes current

**Funding:**
10. Wire instructions from title
11. Signed note + deed of trust

---

## Hot Buttons

- **No condos:** Kiavi has removed DSCR condo eligibility. Don't submit. Go to Lima One for condos.
- **Lease or market rent letter:** Must be one or the other — Kiavi will not accept a borrower's verbal estimate. Order the appraisal with Form 1007 even if occupied.
- **10 property cap:** If borrower has 10+ financed properties, Kiavi will decline regardless of FICO/DSCR. Ask upfront.
- **2–4 unit add-on:** 2–4 unit properties carry a 25bps rate add-on at most tiers.

## Submission Process

1. Submit via Kiavi broker portal (kiavi.com)
2. Kiavi orders their own appraisal (third-party AMC)
3. Conditional approval 5–7 business days from full submission
4. Typical close: 25 days from complete file (longer if condo — but condos ineligible anyway)
