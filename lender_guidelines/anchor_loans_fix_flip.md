## State Restrictions

Anchor Loans operates in: **CA, WA, OR, AZ, NV, CO, TX, FL, GA, NC, SC**

Do not submit properties in other states. This is the most important disqualifier — always check state eligibility first.

Anchor is especially strong in California (their origin market) where they have the fastest close times and deepest relationships with title companies.

---

## Program Differentiator

Anchor Loans is the **highest-loan-cap fix-and-flip lender** in the stack ($20M vs others at $3–5M). For large commercial conversion projects, luxury flips, and multifamily value-add in their states, Anchor is often the only lender who can do the deal size.

They also excel at speed in California — can close in 5–7 business days for experienced borrowers in CA.

---

## Eligible Property Types

SFR, 2–4 unit, warrantable condos, townhomes, 5+ unit multifamily (larger projects).

---

## Borrower Experience

Anchor Loans expects experienced borrowers (1+ completed project minimum). However, with strong FICO (700+) and low LTV (≤ 60% ARV), they will occasionally approve first-timers on smaller deals (< $500K).

---

## ARV Appraisal

Full appraisal required — Anchor does not accept AVMs for ARV. For deals < $300K in markets they know well, they may allow a drive-by/desk review for AS-IS value only; ARV always requires full inspection.

## Hot Buttons

- **State restriction is the first check:** Property in Ohio? Stop. Anchor can't do it. This is non-negotiable.
- **70% ARV hard cap — no exceptions:** Even in CA. Anchor's credit committee enforces this. If deal needs 72% ARV, it won't work here.
- **GC must be in-state licensed:** California requires CSLB license, not just a general contractor certificate. Verify license type against scope.
- **Large deals are their edge:** For a $2M+ luxury flip or multifamily conversion in FL/TX/CA, Anchor often has better terms than smaller fix-and-flip shops.
- **Anchor has fastest close in CA:** If the deal is in CA and borrower is experienced, Anchor should be the first call — they can close faster than Kiavi or Lima One on California deals.
