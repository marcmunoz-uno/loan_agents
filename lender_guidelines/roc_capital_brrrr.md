## Program Overview

Roc Capital's bridge/BRRRR product is more conservative than Kiavi or Lima One on LTV (70% ARV vs 75%). They compensate with experienced-borrower pricing discounts and faster turnaround for repeat borrowers.

Roc Capital excels on larger BRRRR projects ($500K+) where other lenders' $3M maximums are limiting.

---

## Borrower Experience — Required

Roc Capital requires a track record for all bridge/BRRRR loans:
- **Minimum:** 2 completed projects (SFR or 2–4 unit)
- Documentation: prior closing HUDs showing purchase + resale or rental stabilization

No first-time investors. If borrower has 0 experience, route to New Silver (first-timer program) or Lima One (1+ experience tier).

---

## ARV Appraisal — Full Appraisal Only

Roc Capital requires a full licensed appraisal for ARV. They do not accept:
- Broker Price Opinions (BPOs)
- Automated Valuation Models (AVMs) for ARV determination
- Appraiser's "desk review"

This adds 5–7 days to the timeline. Order early.

---

## Draw Structure

- Initial advance: as-is value × 80% or total budget × 85%, whichever is less
- Holdback: renovation budget released in 3–5 draws
- Roc Capital orders draw inspections ($300/draw, borrower pays)
- Renovation budget cap: renovation cannot exceed 75% of as-is value (no ground-up on this program)

---

## Standard Conditions Package

Same as Lima One BRRRR with these additions:
1. Borrower experience documentation (2+ completed projects required)
2. Full ARV appraisal (AS-IS + subject-to-renovation combined)
3. Detailed renovation budget with contractor bids
4. GC license + insurance + W9
5. 3 months reserves (interest carry)

## Hot Buttons

- **70% ARV cap is a hard cap:** Roc will not go to 75% under any circumstance. If the deal requires 75% ARV to work, route to Lima One or Kiavi BRRRR.
- **2-project experience minimum:** The most common decline reason is insufficient experience. Don't submit first-timers — they will be declined.
- **ARV from full appraisal adds time:** Budget 10–14 days for the appraisal. Rush appraisal fee is $400 extra.
- **Renovation budget cap:** If renovation is > 75% of as-is value, it suggests ground-up or near-ground-up work — Roc doesn't do that. Route to a construction lender.
- **Roc's bridge-to-DSCR is seamless:** Like Kiavi, Roc offers a direct refi path into their DSCR rental product at the end of BRRRR. If borrower plans to hold, this is a clean exit.
