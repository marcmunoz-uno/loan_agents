## Program Overview

Kiavi's BRRRR product is a short-term bridge loan designed for the Buy-Rehab-Rent-Refi-Repeat strategy. The loan covers acquisition + renovation. At stabilization (property leased, DSCR ≥ 1.00), the borrower refinances into Kiavi's DSCR rental product.

Kiavi incentivizes the refi-out by offering a rate discount on the DSCR product for borrowers who complete the BRRRR cycle with them.

---

## Eligible Property Types

SFR, 2–4 unit, townhomes. No condos, no 5+ unit, no commercial.

---

## Draw Structure

- Initial advance: purchase price (up to 80% of as-is value or 65% of ARV, whichever is less)
- Renovation holdback: released in draws as work is completed
- Draw inspections required (Kiavi orders inspection — borrower pays $250/inspection)
- Typically 3–4 draws; large projects may have 5–6
- Final draw released at completion + stabilization

---

## AS-IS and ARV Appraisal

Both required. Kiavi orders combo appraisal from their AMC. Do not order your own — Kiavi will not accept it.

## Hot Buttons

- **Must refi into Kiavi DSCR within 12 months:** If borrower intends to take the DSCR loan to a competitor, Kiavi still allows it but loses the cross-sell discount. Document exit strategy.
- **ARV support:** Kiavi is conservative on ARV. Run comparable sales carefully — they will appraise conservatively in tertiary markets. If ARV is tight, have a plan B.
- **Draw inspections:** Budget draw timelines carefully. In hot markets, inspectors book 5–7 days out. Renovation delays that push past 12 months require an extension — $500 fee + rate bump.
- **10 property cap applies here too:** BRRRR program shares the 10-property cap with DSCR.
