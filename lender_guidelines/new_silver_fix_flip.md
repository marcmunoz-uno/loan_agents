## Program Differentiator

New Silver is the fastest fix-and-flip lender for straightforward deals. They offer an **instant online pre-qual** and can issue a term sheet in under 5 minutes for deals that fit their matrix. Close in as few as 7 days for experienced borrowers.

---

## Eligible Property Types

SFR, 2–4 unit, warrantable condos, townhomes, and small multifamily.

Not eligible: Ground-up construction (use separate New Construction product), manufactured, rural, commercial.

---

## Borrower Experience

New Silver is more flexible than Lima One on experience:

- **First-time investors:** Eligible up to 65% ARV / 80% LTC
- **1–2 completed flips:** Up to 70% ARV / 85% LTC
- **3+ completed flips:** Up to 75% ARV / 90% LTC

No experience minimum — first-timers are welcome at the conservative tier.

## Standard Conditions Package

**Submission (mostly automated):**
1. New Silver online application + pre-qual (instant)
2. Purchase contract
3. Renovation budget

**Prior to Close:**
4. AS-IS + ARV appraisal (New Silver orders via API — fast turnaround)
5. Title commitment
6. Insurance binder (builder's risk during construction)
7. GC license + insurance certificate
8. Bank statements (2 months)

---

## Hot Buttons

- **Online-first lender:** New Silver's process is heavily automated. Work through their portal, not email.
- **ARV is the binding constraint:** Their algorithm prices from ARV. Submit deals where you're confident in comps — a low-come appraisal will reduce proceeds, not kill the deal, but it may change the economics.
- **First-timers at 65% ARV:** This is a viable entry point for borrowers who don't yet have a track record. Other lenders decline first-timers entirely.
- **2 months reserves:** Lower reserve threshold than Lima One — good for borrowers with tighter liquidity.
